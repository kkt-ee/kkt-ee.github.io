I am Kishore, currently associated with an ANRF-PAIR sponsored project in the Electrical Engineering department at IIT Bombay. My research focuses on unifying Multiresolution Analysis and Machine Learning for diverse application domains. I submitted my Ph.D. thesis in December 2025, titled "Interpretable and Frugal Learning Systems Employing Multiresolution Pyramids and Volterra Kernels". I am currently open to postdoctoral and research scientist positions.

Research Theme

My R&D aims to unify multiresolution principles/ wavelets/ filter banks/ multi-rate systems/ time-frequency methods with machine learning/ deep learning/ neural networks to analyze one dimensional, two dimensional and higher-dimensional data and make inferences from it.
